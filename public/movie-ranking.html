The Best Movie According to Ekwenze Chino

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

In Pursuit of Happiness

This is a movie that tells the real life story of a single father who chases a career in
in the wallstreet, while having to take care of his son, which his wife abandon to go
away for her her own reason

3Idiot

3 idiot is an Idian Movie that highlight the struggles of Engineering Students in college
the struggle they had to put up with, finding what they are truly passionate about
and the desire to get a good grade to meet the expectation of their family
This movie really means a lot to me as a former students of Engineering. I still play
some of the songs used in the movies till this day.

Still Falling

Still falling is a Nigerian Movie that tells the story of 2 Unniversity lovers who reconnected
after so many years when the lady was embarking on a trip on a private jet to be flown by her
ex back the in the University

During their dating days in the University the boyfriend made life miserable for her
and brought out the beast in her. Now connected she sorts to have her own revenge on her then
who now happens to be born again, and about to follow in the footstep of his Pastor
father.
